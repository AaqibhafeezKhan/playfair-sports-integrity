Our Story

About PlayFair

Dedicated to promoting integrity in sports through education, training, and advocacy since 2018.

Our Mission

Building Trust in Sports Worldwide

PlayFair was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to ensure that integrity remains at the heart of sports at every level. We believe that fair play, ethical conduct, and transparency are essential for the continued growth and success of sports globally.

Through our comprehensive education programs, we have trained over 50,000 athletes, coaches, and sports administrators across 25 countries. Our evidence-based approach combines cutting-edge research with practical, real-world applications.
